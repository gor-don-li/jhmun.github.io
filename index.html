Johnston Heights Model United Nations

February 11th, 2023

Dear Prospective Delegates and Faculty

It is my honour to extend a warm welcome to you all as we prepare to host the second iteration of Johnston Heights Model United Nations Conference. The conference will be held at Johnston Heights Secondary School on February 11, 2024. As the Secretary-General of this event, I am humbled to be a part of this occasion and look forward to seeing you all.

JHMUN serves as a unique platform for students to engage in meaningful discussions and debates centred around current global issues. As young leaders of tomorrow, your participation in this event provides an opportunity to develop critical thinking skills, improve your public speaking abilities, and gain a better understanding of different cultural perspectives. Additionally, it serves as a platform to hone skills that are essential in navigating the world of diplomacy, global citizenship and international relations.

I encourage you all to take full advantage of this opportunity to engage with like-minded individuals who share the same passion for international affairs. As we move forward towards a better and more equitable world, we must equip ourselves with the necessary tools and skills to navigate complex global challenges. We look forward to hosting you and ensuring that you're experience at the JHMUN is both enjoyable and enriching

Yours sincerely,

Gordon Li

Secretary-General
